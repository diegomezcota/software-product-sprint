About myself

Hello! My name is Diego Gómez Cota, you can call me Diegomez. I am a junior Computer Science student at Tecnológico de Monterrey. I am Mexican, love seafood, music (specially techno and hip-hop), and soccer. I come from Sinaloa (north-west coast), I enjoy hot weather and the beach.

I love tech because we can use it as a tool for helping each other. It's incredible that you can relate anything you can imagine to tech and have the ability to deliver a change in those areas. Music, science, and human-interaction are great examples just to name a few.
